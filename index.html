World Population Growth

CS416 Narrative Visualization Project - Usha Bommasamudram

This project shows how world population has grown from year 1951 to 2023, to explain the growth in terms of Total population, Increment of population in number. Used Martini Glass structure for this project. User can analyze the population growth in the last scene by choosing the desired option.
